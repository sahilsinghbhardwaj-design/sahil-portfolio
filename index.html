01 — Who I Am

About Me

Hi! I'm Sahil Singh, a B.Tech Food Technology student at Lovely Professional University and Co-Founder of MindPeak Institute — an online coaching platform helping students crack NEET, JEE Mains, CUET and other Class 12 competitive exams.

At MindPeak, I wear three hats — Co-Founder, HR, and Chemistry Faculty. I handle recruitment and operations while actively teaching Chemistry to students preparing for India's toughest competitive exams. It's early days for our startup, but the vision is big.

Before MindPeak, I worked as a Chemistry Teacher at NNIIT (Sep 2024 – Jan 2025), where I built my teaching foundation and discovered my passion for making complex concepts accessible to students.

Beyond work and academics, I'm increasingly drawn to Data Science, AI, and Machine Learning. I completed my schooling from DAV Senior Secondary Public School, Dehra Gopipur, which gave me a strong academic foundation.

Off the clock, you'll find me on the badminton court or watching cricket. I believe in staying sharp — mentally and physically.
